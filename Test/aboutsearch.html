About us

SEARCH is a global leader in archaeology, maritime archaeology, and the full spectrum of cultural heritage services.

Our staff are cultural resource industry leaders and subject matter experts who support commercial and government clients worldwide. Since 1993, we have completed over 5,000 projects nationwide and across 44 countries, spanning seven continents and four oceans.

By integrating science, technology, and creativity, we harness the power of the past to advance the projects, places, and people we serve.  

SEARCH is a member of the CLS companies—the largest group of land service companies in the United States.

CULTURAL HERITAGE PIONEERS

Health and safety

SEARCH maintains a business culture in which Health and Safety is given a high priority when making business decisions and during active operations. We continuously improve our safety practices, risk controls, and employee development by implementing innovative and proactive safety measures. The result has been an impressive legacy of service and accountability for our clients.
