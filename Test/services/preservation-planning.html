Preservation Planning

Ensuring historic resources will continue to provide value and inspiration.

SEARCH supports clients with historic preservation, conservation, restoration, recordation, and public policy development. We provide expertise to local, national, and international teams seeking to protect and enhance a project’s historic elements. Historic redevelopment incentives and design guidance synthesize preservation, economic opportunity, and community support. We have extensive experience navigating the Secretary of Interior’s Preservation Planning Standards, completing Federal Historic Preservation Tax Incentives documentation and applications, and providing design and peer review. Our multidisciplinary team of architectural historians, historians, archaeologists, and maritime archaeologists consistently and appropriately communicates the benefits of historic preservation through public interpretation of historic resources that is responsive to community needs and concerns.
